AIRnalysis: How does Airbnb impact our lives?

Airbnb's Footprint
Airbnb is an online platform for offering lodging and tourism experiences. Since its launch in 2008, it has quickly put its footprint around the world.
Year: 2007
Eric and Emily
Eric is a hotel manager in New York and he recently got married to Emily.
They would like to travel to Boston for a short trip. They want to try out Airbnb as one of their friends recommended it.
But what should they do to get started?
Travel to Boston

After traveling to Boston
Eric and Emily had a great time in Boston and now they are back in New York.
They happen to have a second apartment for rent. Last tenant has just moved out and they are looking for the next.
'But what if we host an Airbnb instead?' Emily thought to herself.
